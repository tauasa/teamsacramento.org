How do I join?

Three steps from curious to club member.

  1. 01

    Come watch or try a free class

    Show up a few minutes before any class on the Schedule page. Your first class is free and no judogi is required - just loose, comfortable clothes.

  2. 02

    Get fitted for a judogi

    Once you're ready to join, we'll help you order the right size judogi (uniform). Loaner gis are available for your first few weeks.

  3. 03

    Register and set up dues

    Fill out a membership form and set up monthly dues. From there, you're officially part of the club - belt testing and tournaments included.
